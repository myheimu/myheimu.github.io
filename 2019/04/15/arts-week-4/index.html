Algorithm: Leetcode #7. Reverse Integer

https://leetcode.com/problems/reverse-integer/

问题：将32-bit的有符号integer，进行十进制颠倒。

解答：模拟法，注意越界。

Runtime: 4ms, faster than 100.00% of C++
Memory Usage: 8.1MB, less than 99.80% of C++
