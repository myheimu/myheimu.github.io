Algorithm: LeetCode #23. Merge k Sorted Lists

问题：将k个有序的链表归并为一个有序的链表

解答：采用最小堆(priority_queue)持有每个链表的head，然后依次挑选当前最小的head进行归并。

Runtime: 24 ms, faster than 95.65% of C++ online submissions for Merge k Sorted Lists.
